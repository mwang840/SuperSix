Super Six

Welcome to Super Six Chess

Two teams face each other to the death, and it is your job to lead your team to glory!

You're objective is to put the king of your opponents in inescapable danger

Checkmate: If player 1 has cornered player 2's king and player 2 can't move their king out of danger, player 1 wins

Stalemate: A stalemate happens when a player's king has no legal move to make, the game ends as a draw
